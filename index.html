Biography

Hello! my name is Michael Miracco and I am a graduating senior here at North Carolina State University. I study in the College of Education, concentrating in Learning Design and Technology. Upon finishing here at NC State, I intend to enter the career field of instructional design, and strive to work among professionals creating the future of online schooling and instruction. Outside of the classroom, I enjoy skateboarding, cooking, playing music, and spending time with my partner and our German Shephard.

Some of my favorite classes I've taken during my time at NC State are:

    ECI 519: Special Problems in Learning Design and Technology
    ECI 496: Special Topics in Education: Introduction to Instructional Design
    EI 201: Exploring Interdisciplinary Entrepreneurial Thinking
These classes were incredibly engaging, constructive, and helped me learn more about my career path than any class before. Without taking these classes, I'm unsure that I would be as prepared to tackle the workforce as I am now. I am incredibly thankful for the oppurtunities I have been given during my time at North Carolina State University, and I am incredibly excited and hopeful for whatever the future holds for me.
